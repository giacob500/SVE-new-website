During the Roman period, Verona and Valpolicella were already known for the quarrying and processing of their marbles. They were used both for defensive and decorative purposes: the streets, the churches, the buildings, and the monuments of Verona are made of this noble material, and their astonishing beauty still amazes visitors.

Nowadays, Verona hosts one of the most important districts for marble processing, relying on a network of producers and craftsmen of the highest level and competence. STUDIOSVE is part of this story.

Now, STUDIOSVE offers a wide range of gift articles and decorative objects. We have "quarried" more than 2000 different images. Other Italian traditional materials like terracotta and slate were introduced. We customize our products on demand. The only limit is your imagination. {% endblock %}
